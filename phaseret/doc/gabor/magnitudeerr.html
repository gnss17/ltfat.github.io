MAGNITUDEERR - Computes spectral convergence

Usage

E = magnitudeerr(target,reconstructed)

Description

magnitudeerr(target,reconstructed) computes spectral convergence of 2 magnitude spectrograms.

References:

D. Griffin and J. Lim. Signal estimation from modified short-time Fourier transform. Acoustics, Speech and Signal Processing, IEEE Transactions on, 32(2):236--243, Apr 1984. [ DOI ]
