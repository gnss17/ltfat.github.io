Returns
Status code Description
LTFATERR_SUCCESS Indicates no error
LTFATERR_NULLPOINTER At least one of the following was NULL: f, g, c
LTFATERR_BADSIZE Length of the signal L or the length of the window gl was less or equal to 0.
LTFATERR_NOTPOSARG At least one of the following was less or equal to zero: W, a, M
LTFATERR_BADTRALEN L must be bigger of equal to gl and must be divisible by a
LTFATERR_INITFAILED FFTW plan creation failed
LTFATERR_CANNOTHAPPEN ptype does not have a valid value from the ltfat_phaseconvention enum
LTFATERR_NOMEM Indicates that heap allocation failed
